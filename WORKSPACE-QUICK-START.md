## ğŸ¯ Step 1: Create Your First Workspace

1. **Login to TaskFlow**
   - Use your email or Google account

2. **Click the Workspace Selector**
   - Located at the top of the sidebar
   - Shows "Personal Workspace" by default

3. **Click "Create Workspace"**
   - Enter workspace name (e.g., "Marketing Team")
   - Add optional description
   - Click "Create Workspace"

**Done!** You're now the owner of a new workspace.

---

## ğŸ‘¥ Step 2: Invite Team Members

1. **Open Workspace Settings**
   - Click "Workspace Settings" in sidebar
   - Or click the gear icon

2. **Go to Members Tab**
   - Click "Invite Member" button

3. **Enter Member Details**
   - Email address of team member
   - Choose role:
     - **Member** - Can view and edit content
     - **Admin** - Can manage members and settings

4. **Send Invite**
   - Click "Send Invite"
   - Copy the invite link
   - Share via email, Slack, or any messaging app

**Tip:** Invite links expire in 7 days!

---

## ğŸ” Step 3: Understand Roles

### Owner
- Full control over workspace
- Can delete workspace
- Can manage all members
- Can change workspace settings
- **Cannot leave workspace** (must transfer ownership or delete)

### Admin
- Can invite/remove members (except owner)
- Can manage tasks, categories, notes
- Can change member roles (except owner)
- Cannot delete workspace
- Can leave workspace

### Member
- Can view all workspace content
- Can create/edit/delete own tasks and notes
- Can move tasks between categories
- Cannot manage members or settings
- Can leave workspace

## ğŸ”„ Step 4: Switch Between Workspaces

1. **Click Workspace Selector**

   - Click on any workspace in the list
   - Current workspace is highlighted

3. **View Updates**
   - All tasks, categories, and notes update automatically
   - Member count shows in top bar

**Tip:** Your last accessed workspace is remembered!

## ğŸ› Troubleshooting

### "Permission Denied" Error
- Check you're a member of the workspace
- Verify your role has required permissions
- Try refreshing the page

### Invite Link Not Working
- Check link hasn't expired (7 days)
- Verify email matches invite
- Ensure you're logged in

### Not Seeing Updates
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Verify you're in correct workspace

### Can't Remove Member
- Only admins/owners can remove members
- Cannot remove workspace owner
- Check your role permissions
